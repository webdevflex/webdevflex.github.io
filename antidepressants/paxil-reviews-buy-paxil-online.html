Serotonin is what nerve cells use to communicate messages between each other. Too little serotonin means that messages will fail to get sent over. This is what results in a number of mental and emotional conditions. It halts the reuptake process, which allows more serotonin to stay in the brain. This allows Paxil to treat things such as PTSD and depression.
