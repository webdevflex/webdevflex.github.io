OUR COOKIE POLICY

When you visit our websites, use our services, applications, tools, navigate through our advertising links and exchange messages with us (for example, by e-mail), we or our authorized service providers may use cookies, web beacons and Other similar technologies for storing information to enable you to perform your desired actions more efficiently, faster and more safely. This page will give you a better understanding of these technologies and how we apply them on our websites, services, applications and tools. Below are the main points of our policy of using cookies, web beacons and similar technologies, which you should familiarize yourself with. The full text of this policy is presented below.

We offer visitors to our websites a number of functions, services, applications and tools that are available only when using these technologies. You can always block, disable and delete cookies, web beacons and other similar items if your web browser or device allows you to do so. However, in this case, you may not be able to use some of the functions of our websites, as well as some services, applications and tools. In addition, during a session with our websites, you may have to enter your password more often. More information about how to block, turn off and delete cookies, web beacons and similar technologies can be found in the "Help" section of your web browser, built-in program, application or device.

If possible, we try to protect our cookies and similar technologies so that they can be used only by us and (or) our authorized service providers. To do this, we assign them unique identifiers. You may encounter cookies, web beacons and similar technologies used by our authorized service providers, with whom we have entered into agreements to assist us in resolving various issues related to the operation of our websites, or about providing you with our services, applications And tools. We use "session" and "permanent", "our" and "other" cookies.

The above technologies are used by us for the following purposes:

Production need - they ensure the functioning of our websites, services, applications and tools, ensure their security and reliability and allow you to access them.

Efficiency - they are used to assess the performance of our websites, services, applications and tools.

Expanding functionality - they provide you with a wider range of functionality when using our Web sites, services, applications and tools.

Advertising and reach to the target audience - they are used by us or by the advertising companies serving us to provide various content, including advertising, adapted to your interests.

We can cooperate with other companies (service providers) that can host - with our permission - cookies, web beacons and similar technologies on our websites, and also integrate them into our services, applications and tools. These service providers help us in the operation of our websites, applications, services and tools, and also help you to perform the desired actions more efficiently, faster and more safely.

Our websites, services, applications and tools can use the services of third parties, such as ad networks and exchanges, for promotional purposes. These ad networks and exchanges, in turn, can use "other people's" cookies, web beacons and similar technologies to gather the information they need to provide you with relevant services. They can also collect other data about you, such as your device ID, IP address and advertising ID (IDFA), which can be used to customize the advertisements displayed on our and other websites to your best interests.
