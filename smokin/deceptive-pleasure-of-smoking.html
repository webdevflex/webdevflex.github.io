Deceptive pleasure of smoking

Body of a smoker

Each cigarette, you smoke gives a certain amount of nicotine in the body. As a result of biochemical reaction, the nicotine contributes to the increased release of neurotransmitters: dopamine and norepinephrine, regulating the functions of brain and central nervous system. That’s why smoking of the next cigarette, a person experiences a short-term condition, characterized by emotional and moral satisfaction, as well as peace of mind.

Eventually, structural and functional changes occur in the organism, violating the natural ability of the body to release the nicotine, noradrenaline and dopamine. In the body of a smoker, the concentration of nicotine, norepinephrine and dopamine decreases at regular intervals, since the body looses the ability independently to release these substances.

Since, nicotine has a directly effect on the release of dopamine and norepinephrine, only a complete cessation of smoking can guarantee a man the normalization of psychophysiological processes in the body.

This is the fact that nicotine is a drug which causes psychological addiction. Hundreds of millions of people know about the danger of smoking, thousands of people try to give up smoking. And only some of them can get rid of the harmful habit which has become epidemic and is called nicotine addiction.
