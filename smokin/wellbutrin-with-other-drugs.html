Serious and less serious side effects

Serious side effects may include:

  • Severe skin reaction fever, followed by a red or purple skin rash that spreads, swelling in your face, swelling in your tongue, sore throat,burning in your eyes, skin pain.
  • Trouble concentrating, confusion,unusual behavior or thoughts, hallucinations; or
  • Fast heartbeats.
  • Convulsions (seizure);
  • Swollen glands, fever, joint pain, rash, itching;

Less serious side effects may include:

  • Ringing in your ears, dry mouth,stomach pain;
  • Loss of interest in sex;
  • Headache, muscle pain, nausea, dizziness
  • Sore throat;
  • Changes in appetite;
  • Skin rash, increased urination or increased sweating, mild itching
  • Weight loss or gain

Before taking

You shouldn’t take Wellbutrin if you take the next drugs - phenelzine (Nardil), furazolidone (Furoxone), rasagiline (Azilect), isocarboxazid (Marplan),tranylcypromine (Parnate), selegiline ( Emsam,Eldepryl, Zelapar) or in the last fourteen days.

  • Don’t use this drug if you have the next problems with your health:
  • If you are using 2nd form of bupropion; or
  • A seizure disorder or epilepsy;
  • Bulimia or anorexia (an eating disorder);
  • If you have suddenly stopped using sedatives or alcohol.

This drug may cause seizures . Go to your prescriber at onceat consult with him, if you have any medical conditions.

Your prescriber may to order special tests or adjust your drug(Wellbutrin) dosage :

  • A history of seizures, head injury, or spinal or brain cord tumor;
  • High blood pressure,heart disease, history of heart attack;
  • Manic depression (bipolar disorder).
  • Liver disease ; or
  • Kidney disease
